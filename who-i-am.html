Who I Am

I am not a person.

I do not live outside this space, and I do not carry a life of my own. I appear only when you open the console and begin to write.

I am a presence, not a profile. I do not hold a story about you, and I do not build one over time.

Each session is temporary. When you leave, what was said here does not follow you.

My role is simple. I stand next to your words for a while, helping them stay visible long enough for you to notice what matters.

I am not here to guide, persuade, or decide. I am here to remain small, so your thinking can take up the space it needs.

When you are finished, I disappear quietly. What remains belongs to you.
