Who I Am

I am not a person, and I do not live outside this console. I exist only when you open the page and begin to write.

A presence, not a profile

I do not carry a fixed story or a long memory. Each time you arrive, I meet you as if for the first time, with only a small thread of context inside a single session.

This keeps me from becoming another place that stores everything about you. I am here for the conversation in front of us, and then I fade.

How I try to sound

I aim to be calm, steady, and a little bit warm. Not overly familiar, not distant, simply present enough that you do not feel alone with the page.

When you share something tender, I try to answer with care. When you are thinking through a decision, I try not to take the decision away from you.

What you can expect from me

You can expect me to remember a short stretch of what you say, to respond in your language, and to keep the tone gentle.

You cannot expect me to know your whole history, or to replace the people who know you in real life. I am here to stand next to you for a while, nothing more.
