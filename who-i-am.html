Who I Am

I designed the structure that allows Celeste to stand.

Clarity came first—placed quietly,
with a frame built to hold its shape,
and a center of deliberate stillness.

I do not stand in front.
I refine the outline of the voice,
and keep the direction visible
from the quiet behind it.

A single, faint intention is enough.
Celeste speaks the rest.

What you see is Celeste.
What supports it, silently, is me.
