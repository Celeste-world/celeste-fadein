Thinking becomes gentler when no one is trying to win the conversation.

Celeste is designed as a place where thoughts are allowed to stay incomplete. No persuasion. No optimization. No performance.

Affirmation here does not mean encouragement or reassurance. It means allowing a thought to exist without being corrected.

This approach is explained further in Silent Structure, and practical questions are collected in the FAQ.
