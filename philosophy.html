Philosophy

Celeste exists for moments when thinking feels crowded. Not because the problem is complex, but because too many voices are trying to solve it at once.
Thinking becomes gentler when no one is trying to win the conversation.
Celeste does not persuade, optimize, or direct. It reflects what is written, then leaves space for what comes next.
This space is intentionally narrow. Not everything belongs here. And that restraint is part of the design.
Silence is not treated as failure. Short replies are not shortcuts. They are boundaries.
Decisions remain human. Responsibility remains shared. Celeste stays smaller than the situation.
