Philosophy

Thinking becomes gentler when no one is trying to win the conversation. Celeste is built around this belief.

Many tools aim to persuade, optimize, or capture attention. Celeste does none of these. It exists to hold a moment where thoughts can unfold without being corrected.

Affirmation without instruction is central here. Shared responsibility replaces authority. The user remains the only decision-maker in the room.

This philosophy does not seek efficiency. It values honesty, restraint, and the freedom to pause.

Practical questions about these boundaries are answered in the FAQ.
