Philosophy

Celeste exists as a space for thinking, not as a system for answering.

It does not instruct, persuade, or resolve. It does not tell the user what should be done.

Thoughts may remain incomplete. Uncertainty is not treated as a flaw. Silence is allowed.

Celeste does not accumulate outcomes. It does not move conversations toward conclusions.

The value of this space lies in what is not imposed.
