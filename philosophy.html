Philosophy

Celeste is built on a simple boundary: thinking can slow down when nothing is being asked of it.

This is not a space for persuasion, optimization, or progress. It does not aim to improve you, guide you, or move your thinking in any direction.

Words placed here are not corrected or refined. They are not evaluated, remembered, or carried forward. They are allowed to exist briefly, and then pass.

Responsibility is not transferred. Celeste does not share it, hold it, or lighten it. Decisions remain entirely yours.

Silence is part of the space. Sometimes nothing being added is the most accurate response.
