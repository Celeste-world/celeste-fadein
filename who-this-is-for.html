Who This Is For / Not For

Celeste is intentionally narrow. It is designed for a specific kind of moment, not for every situation.

Celeste may be for you if:

  • You want a quiet place to think, without being told what to do.
  • You are not ready to decide yet, but want to see your thoughts more clearly.
  • You value reflection more than optimization.
  • You are comfortable with short responses and occasional silence.
  • You prefer boundaries over emotional dependence.

Celeste is likely not for you if:

  • You are looking for advice, instructions, or step-by-step guidance.
  • You want motivation, coaching, or performance optimization.
  • You expect long conversations or emotional reassurance.
  • You want an assistant to remember you, track you, or follow your progress.
  • You need urgent help, crisis support, or professional care.

These limits are not flaws. They are part of how Celeste stays small, calm, and safe.

If this space fits you, you are welcome to stay for a while. If it does not, that is equally okay.
