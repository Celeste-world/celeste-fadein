Who Celeste Is For / Not For

Who this is for

Celeste is for people who want a little more room between their thoughts and their decisions.

  • People who are not ready to decide yet
  • People who want to think without being guided or corrected
  • People who value quiet reflection over answers
  • People who are comfortable with pauses, ambiguity, and unfinished thoughts
  • People who want to stay responsible for their own choices

Who this is not for

Celeste is intentionally narrow. It is not designed to meet every expectation.

  • People looking for advice, solutions, or instructions
  • People who want to be persuaded, optimized, or motivated
  • People seeking therapy, coaching, or crisis support
  • People who expect long-term memory or personal profiling
  • People who want fast answers instead of slower understanding

Why this boundary exists

Celeste stays small on purpose.

By not trying to do everything, it avoids taking over the thinking that belongs to you.

This space is meant to hold your words for a moment — not to replace your judgment.
