Who Celeste Is For
And Who It Is Not

Who this is for

Celeste is for people who are not ready to decide yet.

It is for moments when thoughts feel unfinished, tangled, or slightly out of reach, and you want to lay them out without being pushed toward a conclusion.

Celeste works best for those who value quiet reflection, gentle mirroring, and the freedom to remain undecided.

Who this is not for

Celeste is not for people looking for answers, solutions, or instructions.

It does not optimize your thinking, motivate action, or replace professional, medical, or emotional support.

If you want fast conclusions, confident direction, or a system that tells you what to do next, Celeste will likely feel too quiet.

Why this boundary matters

Celeste stays intentionally small.

This boundary protects your agency, your pace, and the space where thinking can remain unfinished for a while.

Not everything needs to be resolved. Some thoughts simply need room.
