Who this is for — and who it is not

Celeste may be for you if

  • You want a quiet place to write without being corrected or guided.
  • You are not ready to decide yet, but want to see your thoughts more clearly.
  • You value reflection over speed, and space over answers.
  • You prefer tools that stay small and do not ask for commitment.
  • You are comfortable with pauses, short replies, and unfinished thoughts.

Celeste is probably not for you if

  • You want clear advice, instructions, or step-by-step solutions.
  • You expect an assistant to remember you, track progress, or follow goals.
  • You are looking for motivation, persuasion, or productivity optimization.
  • You need immediate emotional reassurance or crisis support.
  • You prefer long conversations with strong personality or direction.

A quiet boundary

Celeste is intentionally narrow. It does not try to fit everyone, and it does not grow by adding features.

If this space feels too small, that is not a failure. It simply means you are looking for something else — and that is okay.
