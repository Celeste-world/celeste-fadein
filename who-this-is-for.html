Who Celeste Is For — And Not For

Celeste may be for you if

  • You want space to think without being pushed toward a conclusion.
  • You are not ready to decide yet, but want to see your thoughts more clearly.
  • You value calm reflection over speed, optimization, or productivity tricks.
  • You prefer short, gentle responses that leave room for your own thinking.
  • You are comfortable with silence being part of the process.

Celeste is probably not for you if

  • You want clear instructions, step-by-step advice, or definitive answers.
  • You are looking for motivation, coaching, or performance optimization.
  • You expect emotional reassurance or constant engagement.
  • You want an assistant that remembers you, tracks progress, or builds a profile.
  • You need urgent support, diagnosis, or crisis intervention.

Why this boundary matters

Celeste is intentionally narrow. It stays small so that you can stay in control.

This boundary is not a limitation — it is the design. If this space feels right, you will know quickly. If it does not, that clarity is just as valuable.
