Who Celeste Is (and Is Not) For

Celeste is intentionally small. It is designed to support quiet thinking — not to lead, decide, or fix.

This is for you, if…

— you want space to think, not instructions.

— you are not ready to decide yet.

— you prefer reflection over optimization.

— you value clarity that comes from writing things out.

This is not for you, if…

— you want direct advice or answers.

— you expect emotional reassurance or motivation.

— you are looking for therapy, coaching, or diagnosis.

— you need urgency, persuasion, or constant engagement.

Celeste stays smaller than your situation on purpose. That is not a limitation — it is the design.
