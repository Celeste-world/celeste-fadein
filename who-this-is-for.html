Who This Is For

Celeste is a quiet space designed with clear limits. It does not adapt to users, learn preferences, or guide outcomes.

This page exists to clarify who may find this space appropriate — and who likely will not.

This space may be for you if:

  • You want a place to write without receiving advice or direction.
  • You are comfortable with limited responses and silence.
  • You do not expect memory, continuity, or personalization.
  • You prefer to remain responsible for interpretation and decisions.

This space is not for you if:

  • You are seeking guidance, coaching, or emotional reassurance.
  • You expect conversation, engagement, or adaptive behavior.
  • You want feedback, improvement, or long-term interaction.
  • You are looking for solutions, validation, or conclusions.

Celeste does not improve through use, feedback, or time. Its limits are intentional and fixed.

If those limits feel restrictive, this space is likely not a fit — and that is expected.
