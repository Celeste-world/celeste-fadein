Who Celeste Is For
And Who It Is Not

Who this is for

Celeste is for moments when decisions are not ready to be made.

It is for placing unfinished thoughts somewhere neutral, without pressure to organize, resolve, or advance them.

Celeste suits those who are comfortable with thinking remaining incomplete for a while.

Who this is not for

Celeste is not designed to provide answers, plans, or direction.

It does not evaluate your thinking, suggest next steps, or replace professional, medical, or emotional support.

If you are looking for speed, certainty, or instruction, this space may feel too limited.

Why this boundary exists

Celeste remains intentionally small.

These limits protect your agency and pace, and prevent the space from becoming something it is not.

Not all thoughts need solutions. Some only need somewhere to rest briefly.
