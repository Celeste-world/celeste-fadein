FAQ

What is Celeste?
Celeste is a small, quiet console for thinking.

Does Celeste remember me?
No. Each session is temporary.

Is Celeste an AI assistant?
It uses AI, but does not act as an assistant or advisor.

Can Celeste give advice?
No. Decisions always remain with you.

What languages are supported?
You can write in any language. Celeste replies in the same language when possible.
