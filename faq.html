Frequently Asked Questions

This page answers common questions about what Celeste is, how it works, and why it is intentionally designed to stay small.

1. What is Celeste?
Celeste is a small, quiet console for thinking. It gives you space to write and see your thoughts more clearly, without telling you what to do.
2. Is Celeste an AI assistant or a chatbot?
Celeste uses AI, but it is not designed to act like a typical assistant or chatbot. It does not optimize, persuade, or lead conversations.
3. Does Celeste remember me?
No. Celeste does not keep long-term memory or build a profile about you. Each session is temporary.
4. Can Celeste give advice or make decisions for me?
No. Celeste avoids giving direct advice or making decisions on your behalf. Decisions always remain with you.
5. Is Celeste a therapist or coach?
No. Celeste is not a therapist, coach, or professional service. It cannot replace human support.
6. What happens if I talk about something serious or urgent?
If a topic feels unsafe or urgent, Celeste intentionally stays smaller. Human support matters more in those situations.
7. Why are the responses so short?
Short replies leave room for your own thinking. Silence is treated as part of the conversation.
8. Is my data stored?
No accounts, no tracking, no long-term storage. Celeste is designed to be temporary.
9. What languages does Celeste support?
You can write in any language. Celeste will respond in the same language when possible.
10. What is Celeste trying to become?
Celeste aims to stay gentle while becoming wiser. The goal is not to speak more, but to listen better.
