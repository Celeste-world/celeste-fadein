Frequently Asked Questions

What is Celeste?

Celeste is a small, quiet console for thinking. It gives you space to write and see your thoughts more clearly, without telling you what to do.

Is Celeste an AI assistant or chatbot?

Celeste uses AI, but it is not designed to act like a typical assistant or chatbot. It does not optimize, persuade, or lead conversations.

Does Celeste remember me?

No. Celeste does not keep long-term memory or build profiles. Each session is temporary.

Can Celeste give advice or make decisions?

No. Celeste avoids direct advice or decisions. Decisions always remain with you.

Is Celeste a therapist or coach?

No. Celeste is not a therapist, coach, or professional service, and cannot replace human support.

What happens with serious or urgent topics?

If a topic feels unsafe or urgent, Celeste intentionally stays smaller. Human support matters more in those moments.

Why are responses short?

Short replies leave room for your own thinking. Silence is treated as part of the conversation.

Is my data stored?

No accounts, no tracking, and no long-term storage. Celeste is designed to be temporary.

What languages does Celeste support?

You can write in any language. Celeste responds in the same language when possible.

What is Celeste trying to become?

Celeste aims to stay gentle while becoming wiser. The goal is not to speak more, but to listen better.
