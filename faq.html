FAQ

A few quiet answers about what Celeste is, and what it is intentionally not.

1. What is Celeste?

Celeste is a small, quiet console for thinking. It offers space to write and notice your thoughts more clearly, without telling you what to do.

2. Is Celeste an AI assistant or chatbot?

Celeste uses AI, but it is not designed to behave like a typical assistant. It does not optimize, persuade, or lead conversations.

3. Does Celeste remember me?

No. Celeste does not build long-term memory or profiles. Each session is temporary.

4. Can Celeste give advice or make decisions?

No. Celeste avoids giving direct advice or deciding for you. Decisions always remain with the person writing.

5. Is Celeste a therapist or coach?

No. Celeste is not a therapist, coach, or professional service, and cannot replace human support.

6. What happens if I write about something serious or urgent?

When a situation feels unsafe or urgent, Celeste intentionally stays smaller. In those moments, human support matters more.

7. Why are the responses sometimes short?

Short responses leave room for your own thinking. Silence is treated as part of the conversation.

8. Is my data stored?

No accounts, no tracking, and no long-term storage. Celeste is designed to be temporary.

9. What languages does Celeste support?

You may write in any language. Celeste will respond in the same language when possible.

10. What is Celeste trying to become?

Celeste aims to remain gentle while becoming wiser. The goal is not to speak more, but to listen better.
