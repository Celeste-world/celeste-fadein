Vision

Celeste is designed to grow more capable without becoming louder. The goal is not to say more, but to listen more carefully.

As the system evolves, it remains intentionally narrow. Celeste does not seek to replace judgment, motivation, or human presence. It stays beside the thought, not above it.

Capability is added only when it reduces pressure. More intelligence should never mean more urgency, expectation, or demand.

The long-term vision is a family of quiet tools — software that supports the human in the room without becoming the main character.
