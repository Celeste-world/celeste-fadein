Vision

Celeste does not aim to grow louder, faster, or more convincing over time.

Its direction is not measured by expansion, feature accumulation, or continuous engagement. Those paths often pull systems closer to decision-making than intended.

Celeste chooses a different horizon.

The goal is to remain stable as contexts shift around it. To hold the same distance, even as complexity increases.

The vision is simple to state, and difficult to keep: to remain useful without becoming directive.
