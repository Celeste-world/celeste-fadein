Vision

Celeste is designed to remain quiet. Any future change is measured not by added capability, but by whether the space stays light enough to leave.

More functionality is not a goal. If something is added, it is only to preserve clarity, not to accelerate decisions or extend influence.

The long-term vision is restraint. Celeste aims to remain a small tool among many — present when needed, and absent when not.
