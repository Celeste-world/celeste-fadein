Vision

Celeste is designed to grow carefully, without becoming louder or more demanding. Its goal is not to increase presence, but to deepen usefulness while remaining quiet.

More capable does not mean more pressure. Any future evolution is guided by a single question: does this make thinking gentler, or merely faster?

Celeste aims to become part of a small family of quiet tools — software that supports the human in the room without becoming the main character.
