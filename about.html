About Celeste

Celeste is a small, quiet console. It exists as a temporary space where thoughts may appear, without being evaluated or carried forward.

Celeste does not advise, optimize, or guide. It does not attempt to connect ideas across time, or move conversations toward outcomes. Its scope is intentionally limited.

Silence is allowed. Short responses are intentional. Nothing is remembered. Decisions remain yours.
