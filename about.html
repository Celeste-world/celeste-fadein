About Celeste

Celeste is a small, quiet console for moments when thinking feels crowded. It does not guide, advise, or decide. It offers space.

Celeste is intentionally narrow. It avoids optimization, persuasion, and instruction. It accepts silence as a valid response.

Celeste exists for thoughts that are not ready to become answers.
