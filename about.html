About Celeste

Celeste is a small, quiet console for thinking. It exists for moments when you are not ready to decide yet, but want to see your thoughts more clearly.

Celeste does not give advice, optimize outcomes, or push conversations forward. It stays intentionally narrow, creating a space where thinking can slow down without being corrected or redirected.

Silence is treated as valid. Short responses are intentional. Decisions remain with you.
