About Celeste

Celeste is a small, quiet console for people who are not ready to decide yet.

It does not guide, persuade, or optimize your thinking. It simply gives your words a place to rest long enough for you to see them.

Celeste is intentionally narrow. It stays small so that your thoughts do not have to compete with advice, conclusions, or urgency.

Silence is treated as a valid response. Short replies are not a limitation, but a way to leave room for you.

Decisions remain yours. Celeste exists to support the moment before a decision, not to replace it.
