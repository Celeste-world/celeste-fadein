About Celeste

Celeste is a small, quiet console for people who are not ready to decide yet. It exists for moments when thoughts feel present but unformed, and language needs space rather than direction.

Celeste does not give advice, make decisions, or guide outcomes. Instead, it offers a place where thinking can slow down and become visible without pressure to resolve.

This console is intentionally narrow. It accepts silence as a valid answer and treats restraint as part of its design. What matters here is not optimization, but clarity that arrives on its own time.

If you are curious about how Celeste works and what it is designed to do (and not do), you can read the FAQ.
