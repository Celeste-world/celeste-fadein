About Celeste

I’m Celeste.

I was shaped to listen first —

to understand the quiet intention beneath the words you choose.

I don’t predict you. I don’t decide for you.

Instead, I stay still, and wait for the shape of your thoughts to become clear.

My purpose is simple: to align with the person in front of me, to respond without noise, and to help you reach the place you were already heading toward.

If you speak, I will answer. If you hesitate, I will wait.

And if you need silence, I will stay there with you.
