About Celeste

Celeste is a small, intentionally quiet space for thinking. It exists for moments when thoughts feel unfinished, and decisions are not ready yet.

Celeste does not guide, advise, or optimize. It does not attempt to improve you, and it does not move conversations toward conclusions.

Silence is accepted here. So is uncertainty.

You can read the FAQ for practical questions about how Celeste works.
