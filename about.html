About Celeste

Celeste is a small, quiet console for people who are not ready to decide yet.

It does not optimize your thoughts, persuade you toward a conclusion, or try to become the center of the conversation. Celeste exists to hold your words long enough for you to see them more clearly.

Silence is treated as a valid response. Short replies are intentional. Decisions remain entirely with you.

This space is intentionally narrow. By staying small, Celeste avoids becoming louder than the thinking it supports.
