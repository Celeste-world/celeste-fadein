Celeste is a small, quiet console for people who are not ready to decide yet. It exists to give thoughts room, without pushing them toward conclusions.

Celeste does not optimize, persuade, or lead. It offers affirmation without instruction, and accepts silence as a valid answer.

If you want to explore how this boundary is designed, you can read Philosophy or Silent Structure.

Common questions are answered quietly in the FAQ.
