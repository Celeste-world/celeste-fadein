Celeste v4.4A

Fixed Declaration — January 8, 2026

Declaration

Celeste v4.4A is hereby fixed.

This version defines Celeste as a quiet, bounded console — a temporary space where thoughts may appear, without instruction, guidance, or retention.

Celeste does not advise. It does not decide. It does not remember.

It does not grow through use, feedback, or time. Its limits are intentional and complete.

Celeste v4.4A is not a step toward something else. It is the point of rest.

From this declaration forward, Celeste v4.4A remains unchanged.
