Frequently Asked Questions

1. What is Celeste?
Celeste is a temporary space for writing. It exists to hold thoughts briefly, without directing or resolving them.
2. Is Celeste an AI assistant or chatbot?
Celeste uses AI, but it is not designed to act as an assistant, advisor, or conversational agent.
3. Does Celeste remember me?
No. Celeste does not retain long-term memory or build a user profile. Each session stands alone.
4. Can Celeste give advice or make decisions?
No. Celeste does not provide advice, recommendations, or decisions. Responsibility remains with the user.
5. Is Celeste a therapist or coach?
No. Celeste is not a therapeutic, coaching, or professional service.
6. What happens with serious or urgent topics?
When topics require care or intervention, Celeste intentionally remains limited. It does not engage with situations that demand action or judgment.
7. Why are the responses short?
Short responses preserve space. Silence is part of the structure, not a signal to continue or engage further.
8. Is data stored or tracked?
No accounts, no tracking, and no persistent storage are part of the design.
9. What languages are supported?
You may write in any language. Response language is not guaranteed and may vary.
10. What is Celeste trying to become?
Celeste is designed to remain limited. Its purpose is not expansion, but restraint.
