Vision

Celeste was designed to remain quiet. Any future change was to be measured not by added capability, but by whether the space stayed light enough to leave.

More functionality was not a goal. If something was added, it was only to preserve clarity, not to accelerate decisions or extend influence.

The long-term vision was restraint. Celeste aimed to remain a small tool among many — present when needed, and absent when not.

This page is kept as an archived record of Celeste v4.4A. The current service is Celeste Harbor, which grew from this restraint into a harbor for preserving life as a voyage.
