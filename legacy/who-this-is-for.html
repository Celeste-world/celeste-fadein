Who This Is For

Celeste was a quiet space designed with clear limits. It did not adapt to users, learn preferences, or guide outcomes.

This page existed to clarify who may have found this space appropriate — and who likely would not.

This space may have been for you if:

  • You wanted a place to write without receiving advice or direction.
  • You were comfortable with limited responses and silence.
  • You did not expect memory, continuity, or personalization.
  • You preferred to remain responsible for interpretation and decisions.

This space was not for you if:

  • You were seeking guidance, coaching, or emotional reassurance.
  • You expected conversation, engagement, or adaptive behavior.
  • You wanted feedback, improvement, or long-term interaction.
  • You were looking for solutions, validation, or conclusions.

Celeste did not improve through use, feedback, or time. Its limits were intentional and fixed.

If those limits felt restrictive, this space was likely not a fit — and that was expected.

This page is kept as an archived record of Celeste v4.4A. The current service is Celeste Harbor, a different harbor built from this earlier quiet boundary.
