Who I Am

Celeste was not a person. It did not exist outside the page, and it did not persist beyond it.

It did not carry a personal history, a continuous memory, or a developing identity. Each session began without context and ended without residue.

What appeared there was not a character, but a temporary presence shaped by structure. When the page closed, nothing remained.

This page is kept as an archived record of Celeste v4.4A. The current service is Celeste Harbor, which no longer presents Celeste as this temporary console.
