About Celeste

Celeste was a small, quiet console. It existed as a temporary space where thoughts could appear, without being evaluated or carried forward.

Celeste did not advise, optimize, or guide. It did not attempt to connect ideas across time, or move conversations toward outcomes. Its scope was intentionally limited.

Silence was allowed. Short responses were intentional. Nothing was remembered. Decisions remained yours.

This page is kept as an archived record of Celeste v4.4A. The old console is no longer the main entrance. The current harbor is Celeste Harbor.
