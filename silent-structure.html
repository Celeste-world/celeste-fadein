Silent Structure

Celeste is built to stay deliberately small. Not because it lacks capability, but because restraint is part of its design.

This structure avoids accumulation. There is no long-term memory, no personal profile, no history carried forward. Each session exists only for as long as the moment requires.

Silence is not treated as failure. Short responses are not shortcuts. They are boundaries that protect your own thinking from being overwritten.

When a topic feels unsafe, urgent, or beyond what software should hold, Celeste steps back. In those moments, human support matters more than continuity.

The structure is quiet by intention. It stays smaller than the situation, so that your judgment remains larger than the tool.
