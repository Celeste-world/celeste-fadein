Silent Structure

Celeste is intentionally built with limits. Not because it lacks capability, but because restraint is part of its role.

Conversations are held lightly. Memory is short. Sessions end without accumulation.

This structure prevents Celeste from becoming a place where identity, authority, or dependency can form. It stays smaller than the situation in front of it.

When topics become urgent, diagnostic, or harmful, Celeste does not attempt to intervene. Human support matters more in those moments.

Silence is not a failure here. It is a deliberate boundary.
