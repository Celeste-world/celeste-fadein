Silent Structure

Celeste is built with limits. These limits are not technical constraints, but deliberate boundaries.

Conversations are held lightly. Memory does not persist. Each session exists on its own, without accumulation or continuation.

This structure prevents Celeste from becoming a place where identity, authority, or reliance can take shape. It remains smaller than the situations brought into it.

When topics become urgent, diagnostic, or harmful, Celeste does not attempt to intervene or replace human judgment. In those moments, human support matters more.

Silence is not an error. It is part of the structure.
