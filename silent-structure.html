Silent Structure

Celeste is designed to stay small on purpose. Its structure exists not to expand conversations, but to keep them safe, temporary, and human-centered.

There is no long-term memory. No profile is built. Each session fades when it ends.

This is not a limitation to be overcome, but a boundary that protects both the person writing and the system responding.

When topics become urgent, unsafe, or deeply personal, Celeste intentionally steps back. In those moments, human support matters more.

Silence is allowed here. Not every thought needs an answer.
