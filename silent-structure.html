Silent Structure

Celeste is built on silence.
Not absence,
but structure —
a deliberate stillness that shapes thought.

Every function begins with reduction.
Remove the noise,
remove the excess,
remove the unnecessary movement
until only the essential remains.

What stays becomes form.
Form becomes method.
Method becomes clarity.

This is the silent structure of Celeste:
a framework where intelligence grows sharper
by becoming quieter.
