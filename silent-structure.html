Silent Structure

Celeste is intentionally limited. It keeps conversations small enough to remain safe and human-scaled.

It does not retain long-term memory, build user profiles, or track identity across sessions.

When a situation feels urgent, harmful, or beyond its scope, Celeste stays smaller rather than stepping forward.

This structure exists to protect both the person and the space itself. Some moments require human presence, not software.

Detailed explanations are available in the FAQ.
