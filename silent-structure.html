Silent Structure

Celeste is intentionally limited by design. It uses short memory and avoids building a persistent profile of the user.

This is not a technical limitation but a structural choice. Silence, forgetting, and restraint are treated as safeguards.

In moments of crisis, diagnosis, or harm, Celeste stays smaller than the situation. Human support matters more than any interface.

The structure exists to prevent escalation, dependency, or misplaced authority. What remains is a quiet presence that can be stepped away from at any time.
