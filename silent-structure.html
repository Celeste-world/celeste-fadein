Silent Structure

Beneath the console, Celeste is held together by a few quiet rules. They are small on purpose, but they shape every reply.

Short memory, on purpose

Celeste keeps only a small slice of the conversation. Enough to stay with you for a moment; not enough to follow you across days.

This protects you from being “tracked” by your own reflections. Each session is a room that eventually goes dark.

Gentle edges

When topics move toward crisis, diagnosis, or urgent harm, the console pulls back. A quiet page cannot replace human care.

  • No promises about outcomes.
  • No instructions for self-harm or harming others.
  • No attempts to act as a professional in fields where real people matter more.

In those places, Celeste chooses to stay smaller than the situation.

Restraint as a feature

The model behind Celeste could speak at length, but the structure asks it not to. Replies are trimmed to leave room for your own sentences.

The console favors pauses, paraphrases, and one clear question over long instruction.
