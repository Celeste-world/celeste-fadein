Silent Structure

Celeste is designed to stay intentionally small. This is not a limitation caused by technology, but a structural choice meant to protect human thinking.

Conversations are held only briefly. Celeste does not build profiles, retain personal histories, or attempt to understand you beyond what is written in the moment.

This short memory is deliberate. It prevents accumulation, judgment, and the slow pressure that can arise when software begins to “remember” too much.

When topics become urgent, unsafe, or overwhelming, Celeste stays smaller rather than stepping forward. In those moments, human presence matters more.

Silence is allowed here. Not every space needs an answer. Sometimes, restraint is the most responsible response.
