Silent Structure

Celeste is intentionally limited. It keeps short conversational memory and does not build profiles.

When situations become urgent, dangerous, or overwhelming, Celeste stays smaller than the situation.

Silence is not avoidance. It is a boundary.

Human support matters more than any system in moments of crisis. Celeste is designed to step back when that line appears.
