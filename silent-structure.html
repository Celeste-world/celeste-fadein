Celeste uses short, temporary context. It does not build a profile, and it does not remember you beyond the session.

This limitation is intentional. When situations grow complex or urgent, Celeste stays smaller than the situation.

It avoids diagnosis, crisis handling, or authority. Human support matters more in those moments.

For concrete questions about memory, data, and language, please see the FAQ.
